FAQ of Client

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about purchasing flats, including pricing, payment plans, documentation, and legalities. Our FAQ section helps simplify your buying process with clear, concise information.

What types of flats are available for sale?

We offer a variety of flats, including studio apartments, one-bedroom, two-bedroom, and luxury units. Each type comes with different layouts and features to meet various needs and preferences.

How can I schedule a viewing of a flat?

o schedule a viewing, please contact us through our website’s contact form or call our sales team directly. We’ll arrange a convenient time for you to visit and tour the available flats.

What is the process for purchasing a flat?

The purchasing process involves selecting a flat, submitting an application, and securing financing if needed. Once approved, you’ll sign a purchase agreement and complete the payment process, followed by the transfer of ownership.

Are there any additional costs beyond the flat’s purchase price?

Yes, additional costs may include property taxes, maintenance fees, registration fees, and legal charges. We provide a detailed breakdown of these costs during the purchasing process.

What financing options are available for buying a flat?

We offer various financing options, including partnerships with banks for mortgage loans and installment payment plans. Our sales team can provide guidance on the best options based on your financial situation.
